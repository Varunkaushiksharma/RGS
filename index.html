Our Story
A School Built on Trust & Values

We believe every child in the village deserves the very best education — taught with care, in their own language. Our school has been a pillar of this community for over 15 years.

From Class 1st to 10th, we prepare students for their UP Board exams with strong Hindi medium teaching, disciplined study habits, and a warm, family-like environment.

  • Hindi medium from Class 1st to 10th
  • UP Board exam preparation for Class 9 & 10
  • Dedicated and experienced teaching staff
  • Safe, clean, friendly environment
  • Affordable fees for every family
